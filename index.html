Antioquia Unveiled: Discover Medellín, Guatapé, Santa Fe de Antioquia, and Jardín - The Crown Jewels of Colombia!

Medellín: Known as the "City of Eternal Spring," Medellín is a vibrant metropolis surrounded by lush green mountains.

Guatapé: A charming town famous for its colorful zócalos (ornate baseboards) adorning the facades of its buildings. Climb the 740 steps of the iconic El Peñol Rock for breathtaking views of the surrounding lakes and islands.

Santa Fe de Antioquia: Step back in time by visiting this well-preserved colonial town. Wander through cobblestone streets lined with whitewashed buildings adorned with vibrant flowers.

Jardín: Nestled in the verdant coffee region, Jardín is a picturesque town known for its scenic beauty. Stroll through the main square with its colorful houses and charming church. Don't miss a ride on the traditional Willys Jeeps to reach the stunning Cueva del Esplendor waterfall.
